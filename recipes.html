Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • 50ml ofyour whiskey of choice
  • 30ml fresh lemon Juice
  • 20ml simple syrup
  • 20ml egg whites
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • plenty of ice cubes

Method

  1. Add ice to your glass to chill
  2. Place all ingredients, except ice, inside shaker
  3. Seal shaker and shake vigorously for 10 seconds
  4. Pop open shaker and half fill with ice
  5. Seal, and shake well, again
  6. Open shaker and double strain into your glass
