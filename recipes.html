Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • 50ml of your whiskey of choice
  • 30ml fresh lemon Juice
  • 20ml simple syrup
  • 20ml egg whites
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • plenty of ice cubes

Method

  1. Add ice to your glass to chill
  2. Place all ingredients, except ice, inside shaker
  3. Seal shaker and shake vigorously for 10 seconds
  4. Pop open shaker and half fill with ice
  5. Seal, and shake well, again
  6. Open shaker and double strain into your glass

Espresso Martini

Ingredients

  • 50ml vodka or whiskey
  • 50ml strong coffee
  • 20ml Kahlua or Tia Maria
  • 15ml simple syrup or Monin vanilla syrup
  • ice cubes

Method

  1. Make a cup of strong coffee,
    whichever way you like at home
  2. Combine ingredients into shaker,
    be careful not to scald yourself while measuring out the hot coffee
  3. Seal shaker firmly, and shake well
  4. Double strain into a glass without ice

Pornstar Martini

Ingredients

  • 30ml vodka
  • 50ml prosecco
  • 15ml Monin vanilla syrup
  • 1 passionfruit
  • 15ml fresh lime juice
  • 25ml egg white
  • ice cubes

Method

  1. Cut the passionfruit at a quarter of its length,
    and scoop out the fruit into the shaker
  2. Combine all ingredients into shaker, except the prosecco and ice
  3. Shake well, and then shake again with ice
  4. Double strain into a glass with no ice
  5. Top with the prosecco
  6. garnish with the quarter length passionfruit
