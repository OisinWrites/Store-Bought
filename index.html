Ina Rosenberg Garten, American author, host of the Food Network program
Barefoot Contessa, and a former staff member of the White House Office of Management and Budget
made popular the phrase, "if not, store bought is fine."

Drinking culture has taken a swing towards the off-trade market, particularly
because of the global pandemic. A preference for many, to be able to sit in the comfort of
your own home or garden (in that famous Irish weather); the at home boozer
has the joy of a lighter cost on the pocket, but a limited selection available to them
compared to the range boasted in pubs and bars across the country.

Through this site we want to help you upgrade your home bar with
a broader knowledge and confidence to turn that dusty bottle of hooch
at the back of the cabinet into moments to remember, or maybe even
to forget!
